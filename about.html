We Eliminate Manual Document Work.

Azura AI is a specialist AI automation agency that builds pipelines to automate document processing for healthcare, fintech, and logistics companies — replacing slow, error-prone manual workflows with AI that runs 24/7 at 99.9% accuracy.

🎯

Our Mission

To give operations teams back their time. We build AI systems that handle the repetitive, high-volume document work — so your people can focus on the decisions that actually require human judgement.

What We Do

We specialise in Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) — using OCR, Vision AI, and large language models to extract structured data from invoices, insurance claims, KYC documents, customs forms, and medical records. Full ERP and system integration included.

Healthcare providers processing insurance claims. Fintech companies verifying KYC documents. Logistics companies handling customs and shipping paperwork. Any operations team drowning in document processing and looking for a way out.
