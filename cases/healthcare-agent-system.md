# Healthcare Assistant: Patient Pre-Visit Synthesis & Scheduling
> Freeing clinicians from administrative burden with 24/7 autonomous phone and chat agents.

## The Challenge
Doctor burnout is at an all-time high. A significant portion of their day is spent on "Document Prep"—reading through pages of previous visits and lab results to prepare for a 15-minute appointment.

## The Solution: Nexus Patient Agent
An agentic system that sits between the patient and the EMR (Electronic Medical Record). It collects pre-visit data, symptoms, and updates, and synthesizes it into a 1-page "Prep Sheet" for the doctor.

### Core Workflows
- **Autonomous Scheduling**: Handles complex reschedules and cancellations via natural voice/text.
- **Document Synthesis**: Digests previous 12 months of lab work into a trend-analysis report.
- **Patient Triage**: Basic AI-assistive routing based on symptom urgency.

| Benefit | Clinical Impact |
| :--- | :--- |
| Admin Time | Reduced by 4 Hours/Week per MD |
| Patient Satisfaction | 45% Higher (Instant Responses) |
| Appointment No-Shows | Down 30% via AI-Followups |
| Care Quality | Higher (Data-Driven Preps) |

## Compliance & Security
Built on **HIPAA-Compliant infrastructure** with localized, secure inference. No patient data is used for training public models.

## Result
A specialized cardiology clinic **increased their patient capacity by 20%** without hiring a single additional administrative staff member.
