### Step 1: The "Invisible Loss" Calculation
Identify their current manual overhead.

1.  **Employee Salary**: [e.g., $3,500/month]
2.  **Overhead (Benefits, Taxes, Desk Space)**: [Salary x 1.3 = $4,550/month]
   - *Pro Tip: Most business owners forget the overhead cost of a desk and management time.*
3.  **Error Rate Loss**: [Manual tasks have a 2-5% error rate. If an error takes 2 hours of senior management time to fix at $100/hr, that’s $200 per error.]
   - Assume 10 errors per month: **$2,000/month loss.**
4.  **Operational Bottleneck**: How much revenue is "stalled" because data isn't processed fast enough? (e.g., invoices not sent, leads not called).
   - Assume 10% revenue lift from real-time processing: **$[X] / month gain.**

**Total Manual Cost: $6,550+ / month (minimum).**
